Displacement & Hurdle Rate

The group-versus-transient decision. A discounted group booking looks like guaranteed volume — but on a high-demand date it displaces higher-rate transient business you'd have sold anyway. This models the opportunity cost and returns the hurdle rate: the minimum group rate that still beats walking away. Accept volume only when it clears what it crowds out.
